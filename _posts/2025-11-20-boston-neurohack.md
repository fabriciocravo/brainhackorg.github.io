## Summary

The Boston Precision Brain & Mental Health Hack is a three-day collaborative event bringing together researchers, clinicians, and data scientists to work on projects advancing precision psychiatry and computational mental health through neuroimaging and open science practices.

**Dates:** October 28-30, 2025  
**Location:** Northeastern University, Boston, MA
